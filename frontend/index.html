DIAGNOSTIC ARCHIVE

Dataset: MIT-BIH Arrhythmia Database (Moody & Mark, 2001) · 48 half-hour Holter recordings · 360 Hz. 5-class annotation: N (Normal), S (Supraventricular), V (Ventricular), F (Fusion), Q (Unknown/Paced). Waveforms below are representative samples extracted during EDA (notebook 01_eda.ipynb) and saved to data/processed/reference_waveforms.npz. SMOTE oversampling applied on training split only to address severe class imbalance.

NORM · N F1 ~99%

Normal Sinus Rhythm

Regular P-QRS-T · narrow QRS · upright T-wave.

SVE · S F1 ~88%

Supraventricular Ectopic

Narrow QRS · aberrant P-wave morphology · 3% of dataset.

VE · V F1 ~95%

Ventricular Ectopic

Wide bizarre QRS · no preceding P-wave · 6% of dataset.

FB · F F1 ~82%

Fusion Beat

Hybrid N+V morphology · rarest class at 0.7% · hardest to classify.

UNK · Q F1 ~91%

Unknown / Paced

Paced beats or unclassifiable morphology · 7.3% of dataset.
